Contact us

Email aakashdhawan23+fourwinds@gmail.com for support, feedback, or feature requests.

When reporting a problem, please include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Quick troubleshooting

  • Make sure FourWinds is updated to the latest App Store version.
  • Close and reopen the app if a game becomes unresponsive.
  • Restart your device if the problem continues.
  • Include a screenshot in your email when it helps explain the issue.
