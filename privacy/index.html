Privacy Policy

Effective June 19, 2026

Overview

FourWinds is designed to respect your privacy. The current version of the app does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information collected by the app

FourWinds does not collect personal information, gameplay data, location information, device identifiers, advertising data, or analytics data.

Game settings and preferences may be stored locally on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising and third parties

FourWinds does not display third-party advertising and does not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Support communications

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, and description of the issue. We use this information only to respond to your request and provide support.

Children's privacy

FourWinds does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal information from any user, it does not create profiles of children or use their data for advertising.

Changes to this policy

FourWinds may add online multiplayer, additional rulesets, or other features in future updates. If a future version changes how information is handled, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before those changes take effect.
